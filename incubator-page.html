Fundraising Roadmap

  • Grants

    Round Overview
    Flexible, milestone based funding which is non-dilutive with small ticket sizes

    Participating Investors
    Ecosystems and foundations

    Expectations
    Enabling you to build an effective MVP on the investors platform or integrate your project with their ecosystem

  • Angel

    Round Overview
    Hands on investors with extensive experience in your field. Leveraging their networking effect with small ticket sizes

    Participating Investors
    Angels

    Expectations
    A clear vision for the project's future with strong market fit and a compelling value proposition

  • Pre-Seed

    Round Overview
    Focus on validating your concept and building a functional prototype. Moderate ticket size

    Participating Investors
    KOLs, HNIs, VCs, Angels, Syndicates

    Expectations
    Metric driven investors expecting initial traction metrics and a clear path to achieving product-market fit

  • Seed

    Round Overview
    Focus on scaling your product and acquiring initial users. Ticket sizes are larger than pre-seed

    Participating Investors
    VCs, Family Offices, Syndicates

    Expectations
    Strong traction metrics demonstrating product-market fit, a growing user base, and a solid understanding of the competitive landscape

  • Series A

    Round Overview
    Focused on rapid growth and market expansion. Ticket sizes are significantly larger than seed rounds

    Participating Investors
    VCs, Family Offices

    Expectations
    Proven and scalable business model, a dominant market position or clear path to achieving it, and a highly experienced team capable of executing the growth plan
