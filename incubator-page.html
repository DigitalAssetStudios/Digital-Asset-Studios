Fundraising Roadmap

Grants

Round Overview

Flexible, milestone-based funding which is non-
dilutive with small ticket size

Participating Investors

Ecosystems and foundations

Use Cases

Grants are used to conduct detailed research of
novel ideas, start an early adopter community
and begin building the MVP. Grantors may expect
integration into their ecosystem

Angel

Round Overview

Hands on investors with extensive experience in your field.
Leveraging their networking effect with small ticket sizes

Participating Investors

Angels (Ex VC, PE, founders with successful exits, etc)

Use Cases

Hiring a small core team,
complete the legal structuring and MVP / Alpha
and begin testing on early adopters

Pre-Seed

Round Overview

Metric driven investors expecting initial traction metrics
and a clear path to achieving product-market fit.
Typically moderate ticket sizes with a total raise of under $2m

Participating Investors

KOLs, HNIs, VCs, Angels, Syndicates

Use Cases

Scale your team, launch Alpha / Beta and focus on validating your concept/MVP with a growing community of early adopters

Seed

Round Overview

You have demonstrated traction metrics, product-market fit,
a growing user base, and a robust understanding of
the competitive landscape. Total raise is typically under $6m

Participating Investors

VCs, Family Office, Syndicates

Use Cases

Scale product through new features, begin acquiring users,
perform smart contract security audits, reach product-market fit,
prepare tokenomics (if applicable) and launch the product

Series A

Round Overview

You have a proven and scalable business model,
a dominant market position or clear path to achieving it,
along with a highly experienced team capable of executing.
Total raise is typically $10m or above

Participating Investors

VCs, Family Office

Use Cases

Focus is on efficient customer acquisition for rapid growth and market expansion, product feature enhancements and strong profitability
